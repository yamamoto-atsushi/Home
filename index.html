Photo Backup App

Welcome to the Photo Backup App! This application allows you to seamlessly upload photos and videos to a designated Google Drive folder and automatically manage and organize your media files.

Features

  • Automatic backup of photos and videos to Google Drive
  • Organize and manage your media files
  • Easy sharing with friends and family

How to Use

To use the Photo Backup App, follow these steps:

  • Create a shared folder in Google Drive.
  • Use the app interface to upload photos and videos to the shared folder.
  • The app will automatically organize and manage the files for you.
